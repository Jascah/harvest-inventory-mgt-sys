Features

  • Dashboard: Overview of key statistics and harvest trends.
  • Inventory Management: View, edit, and delete harvest inventory items.
  • Add New Harvest: Add details of new harvest items.
  • Storage Management: Manage storage and monitor storage capacity.
  • Reports: Generate reports based on harvest data.
  • Alerts: Receive notifications for inventory and storage updates.
  • Suppliers: Manage supplier information, including contact details and addresses.
